About VeriFy

VeriFy is your trusted companion in the fight against misinformation. Our platform uses advanced AI technology to analyze news articles and help users identify potential fake news.

Our Mission

To empower users with the tools and knowledge needed to combat the spread of misinformation in the digital age, with a special focus on the Malaysian context.

Key Features

  • AI-powered news analysis
  • Real-time verification
  • Malaysian context awareness
  • Educational tips and guidance
  • Detailed statistical insights
